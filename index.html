About us

Phoenix-born and neighbor-proud

SunValley Landscaping started with one truck and a commitment to treating every yard like it was our own. Two decades and over 1,400 projects later, we're still a family business — our kids grew up here just like yours.

We know the heat. We know the soil. We know which plants thrive from Scottsdale to Laveen. Every estimate is free, every crew member is background-checked, and we won't disappear after the job is done.
